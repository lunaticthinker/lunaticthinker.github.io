So we talk! Words after words flowing to the mid of night…

under the watch of the bitter of the Moon

I’m like an open book, until, each time,

we get to wonder the past;

And once again I gaze into the green…

Wish we could remain in the mid of the night,

shell of a moment!

<span style="color: #888888;">Nu ne mai vegheaza decat Luna Amara…<br /> Incep sa iti par o carte deschisa, pana cand, de fiecare data<br /> ajungem sa ne minunam cat timp a trecut;<br /> iar eu ma pierd fara sa vreau in verde…</span>

<span style="color: #888888;">Am putea ramane, oare, blocati in miezul de noapte…<br /> cochilie a  unei clipe.</span>
